Early Life

Sadly, when Fred was only 4 years old, his dad passed away. Thankfully, Smith Senior had enough money to keep his family safe. Smith was then raised by his mom and uncles. Fred Smith was also born with a disease called Calve Perthes disease which is a peculiar form of childhood arthritis of the hips caused by a temporary loss of blood supply to the hip. This made it so that Smith was on braces and crutches most of his early life. However, by the age of 10, Smith outgrew the disease. While Smith excelled at school, his true passion was flying.
